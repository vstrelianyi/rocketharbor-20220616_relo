Gold Coast

Representing the utmost in convenience and elegance, the well-known Gold Coast neighborhood is located just one mile from the Loop with access to magnificent shopping, world-class dining, and a short walk to Oak Street beach and Lake Michigan. High-rise condominiums with rooftop patios overlook Chicago’s Gold Coast lakefront. There are also low-rise residential blocks inland that are just a few steps away from Chicago’s Magnificent Mile. Some of the most notable celebrities enjoy living in the Gold Coast of Chicago.
