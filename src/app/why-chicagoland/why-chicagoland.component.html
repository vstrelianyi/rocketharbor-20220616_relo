Chicagoland: Something for everyone

The Chicagoland region is made up of over 300 suburbs circling around the city of Chicago. The city of Chicago is a diverse area with more than 77 unique neighborhoods. Here you’ll find world class entertainment, museums, dining, public transportation, and more. With so much to offer, there’s something for everyone — we know we can find the perfect place for you.

The Highlights

Conde Nast repeatedly puts Chicago on their Best Big City in the US list for a reason. The city offers great public transportation, more than 80 James Beard Award-winning restaurants and chefs, 26 miles of lakefront trails with 15 miles of beaches. Beyond that, many of Chicagoland’s suburbs have been rated in the Top 100 for lifestyle and educational opportunities.

Culture, lifestyle and entertainment.

Chicago is known for its wonderful culture and recreation opportunities, the food and dining scene, live entertainment, and shopping along the famous Magnificent Mile. The city has 200 live theaters, 50 world-class museums, and free access to the renowned Lincoln Park Zoo.

Another job seeker moving with you?

Chicago has one of the world’s largest and most diversified economies — no single industry makes up more than 14% of the economy, making it easier to find jobs in a lot of different areas of work. Chicago O’Hare airport regularly is one of the ost connected airports in the country, and the world, which makes it a great location for business travelers. All of this is part of why Chicago has been rated the #1 top US city for corporate relocations and expansions for close to 10 years.

Education opportunities

Chicagoland has nationally recognized elementary and high school districts, as well as world-class colleges and universities. Learn more about Illinois State Board of Education.

Transportation

Public: The Chicago Transit Authority (CTA) is the city’s public bus and train system. Metra Commuter Rail System is a series of train lines that connects the suburbs to the city.
Airports: O’Hare International Airport is regularly one of the most connected airports in the world. It’s also accessible by the CTA Blue Line. Midway International Airport is accessible by the CTA Orange Line.
